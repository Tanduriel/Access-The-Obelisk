Access the Obelisk V 0.5.

Warning! This is a test version of the mod. Not all game features are implemented yet, and bugs, unfinished behavior, inconveniences, and other issues are possible. The mod is in active development and will be updated.


Installation:

Simply place all files from the archive into the game folder. Done, you can launch the game.


Controls:

Arrow keys - menu navigation
Enter - select
Home - go to the first item in a menu or list
End - go to the last item in a menu or list
Escape - close a popup, alert or panel (where available)

In game settings:
Up and down arrows - move through tabs and items
Left and right arrows - change slider and dropdown list values
Enter - toggle on or off, confirm language selection

Mod accessibility settings:
Ctrl+m - open or close the mod's own settings menu
Inside it: up and down arrows - move between settings, Enter or space - toggle the setting, Escape - close
Available settings: detailed map descriptions, announcing cards played by enemies, repeating a single item's info

On the map:
Left and right arrows - select an available location, Enter - move
Ctrl+up and down arrows - preview the map forward and backward along the route
Ctrl+left and right arrows - while previewing, switch between branching route options

In combat:
Up and down arrows - move through zones: cards, enemies, heroes
Left and right arrows - move within the selected zone
Enter - play a card, select a target, assign the amount of energy to spend on playing a card
Ctrl+up and down arrows - read detailed information about the selected card, enemy, or hero line by line
Ctrl+home and end - jump to the first or last detail line
Ctrl+left and right arrows - switch to the event buffer and back
f - view the draw pile
Shift+f - discard pile
Ctrl+shift+f - vanish pile (cards removed from the game)
Space - end turn (game key)
Ctrl+e - current energy
Ctrl+t - turn order in the current round (heroes and enemies, depends on speed)
Ctrl+h - health of the focused or active character
Shift+h - health of the hero whose HP is currently lowest
Ctrl+shift+h - health of every hero, from lowest to highest
Ctrl+b - block / shield
Ctrl+f - effects
Shift+v - resistances, immunities and vulnerabilities
Ctrl+i - view enemy cards that they are going to play
Ctrl+r - hear the current round number

Character info panel (opened with i, see below):
Ctrl+left and right arrows - switch to the previous or next hero
Left and right arrows (no Ctrl) - switch between tabs
Ctrl+up and down arrows - read the focused detail line by line
Ctrl+home and end - jump to the first or last detail line
Escape - close the panel

Tome of Knowledge (opened with b, see below):
Ctrl+f - focus the search field
Left and right arrows (no Ctrl) - previous or next page
Ctrl+left and right arrows - previous or next section
Ctrl+up and down arrows - read the focused card or detail line by line
Ctrl+home and end - jump to the first or last line
Escape - close search, close card detail, or close the Tome

Hero selection screen:
Ctrl+Enter - open the selected hero's stats popup
Ctrl+s - open the selected hero's skins popup
Ctrl+b - open the selected hero's cardbacks popup
Shift+left and right arrows - adjust a Madness action's value, or (in the party zone) change which player owns a slot

Give / resource transfer window (multiplayer):
Left and right arrows - change the amount by 1
Shift+left and right arrows - change the amount by 20
Ctrl+left and right arrows - change the amount by 100

Quest and quest item tracking:
Ctrl+shift+up and down arrows - move through the tracked list
Ctrl+shift+home and end - jump to the first or last tracked item
Ctrl+shift+left and right arrows - change category (all, items, quests)

Global keys, or almost global:
Ctrl+g - gold
Ctrl+d - dust
Ctrl+s - supplies
Ctrl+m - open the mod settings menu
b - open the Tome of Knowledge
i - open the character info panel
p - open the multiplayer players list (mute/unmute)

Multiplayer chat:
/ - focus the chat input box
[ - read the previous chat history entry
] - read the next chat history entry
Enter - send the message
Any arrow key while typing leaves the chat box and returns to list navigation

Text fields in alerts and dialogs:
Ctrl+c - copy the field's text to the clipboard
Ctrl+v - paste text from the clipboard into the field


Gamepad:

The gamepad works alongside the keyboard and reproduces the shortcuts above with button combinations. Basic navigation works without holding anything:
D-pad or left stick - arrow keys
A - Enter
B - Escape
Start - Space (end turn)
Select/View - Ctrl+Enter (hero details popup / confirm action)
Select + left or right on the D-pad/stick - browse chat history ([ and ])
Right stick - moves through the event buffer, like Ctrl+arrows

Hold the left trigger for information about your own party and resources:
A - health, B - block/shield, X - energy, Y - effects
Left shoulder - dust, right shoulder - supplies

Hold the right trigger for information about the run and the enemies:
A - gold, B - round number, X - resistances, Y - enemy intents
Left shoulder - turn order, right shoulder - lowest-HP hero

It is not recommended to enable hotkeys in the game settings. The mod interacts with them poorly, and their usefulness is questionable anyway, for example opening the deck with Control.
If the game does not start or freezes at some point, this is not a mod problem. In 99% of cases it is a game issue or an incompatibility with your hardware.
You can enjoy the game. Or suffer, if I messed something up somewhere, or if it is too hard)
